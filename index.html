About

She is a Clinical Psychologist, Educator, and CEO of Smiles In Milez & SIM Events. She is dedicated to promoting mental health awareness, personality development, and leadership training. Her work focuses on helping individuals and organizations achieve growth through stress management, emotional intelligence, and personal growth. Dr. Padmakshi has received several prestigious awards, including theDr. APJ Abdul Kalam Award and the Nadaprabhu Kempegowda Award. Her commitment continues to inspire and positively impact lives worldwide.
